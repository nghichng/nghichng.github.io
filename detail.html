4 school houses Howgarts

Students have the opportunity to join these 4 houses based on the classification of witches' hats

Gryffindor

The Gryffindor family focused on courage, chivalry and boldness. The iconic animal of this house is a lion; colors are magenta and yellow. Near-lose-head Nick is the house ghost. The founder is Godric Gryffindor. This is said to be the best dorm in Harry Potter and a longstanding rival of the Slytherin family.

  • Established:

    Gordic Gryffindor

  • Date:

    XI

  • Tags:

    Bravery,The god of martial arts ,Boldness,

Slytherin

The Slytherin family emphasizes ambition, cunning, leadership and resourcefulness. The Sorting Hat has said that the Slytherin witch will do whatever it takes to get what they want. The symbol of the house is a snake, the main colors are green and silver .

  • Established:

    Salazar Slytherin

  • Date:

    XI

  • Tags:

    Ambitious,Insidious,Resourceful,

Hufflepuff

Hufflepuff, founded by Helga Hufflepuff, emphasizes hard work, loyalty, assertiveness, patience, friendship and good play, not the individual talent. The animal symbol for the house is mezzanine, canary yellow and black is the color of the house.

  • Established:

    Helga Hufflepuff

  • Date:

    XI

  • Tags:

    Adroit,Loyal,Assertive,

Ravenclaw

Ravenclaw emphasizes intelligence, knowledge, creativity and a sense of witty humor. The iconic animal of the house is the falcon, the main colors are navy blue and copper (in the movie, blue and silver)

  • Established:

    Rowena Ravenclaw

  • Date:

    XI

  • Tags:

    Clever,Intellectuality,Creativity,
